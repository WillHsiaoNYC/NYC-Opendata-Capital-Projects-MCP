Managing agencies

Who is building. A line's managing agency is the builder of record — DPR runs many in-house, while DDC and EDC manage on Parks' behalf. The same project can appear under two agencies when work is split.

About this report. Scope: Parks & Recreation budget lines with current budget ≥ $50M, keyed on (managing agency, FMS ID). Category derived from category_dim. Budget and spend from lifetime_budget_variance and the latest budget_history snapshot, reporting period 202601. Cumulative Δ = current budget − original adopted budget; a few lines have very small original budgets, which inflates the change figure. Budget lines are not the same as projects (PIDs); one line can fund several schedules.
